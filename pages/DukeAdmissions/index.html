OUR RICH HISTORY

Duke University was founded in part by James Buchanan Duke, aka big tobacco daddy. The land was taken from Native Americans in order to build these gorgeous gothic buildings that are totally not modeled off of Princeton.

Duke begs to ask the question: "What if Conneticut was in the South?" Taking on inspiration from Ivy Leagues of similar caliber (according to rankings by US news and world reports), Duke has recently introduced QuadEx. In this system, administration can pretend that they are ruling over a world of wizards, similar to the effects of the Harry Potter series.
